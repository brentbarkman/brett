## Patterns in the failures

Common reasons for cut/replace:

- **Focal-element pile-on**: cabins, single trees, lighthouses, fence posts, antenna towers, boats — singular bright/dark elements pull the eye away from content cards. Eight images failed for this reason.
- **Bright sun/moon in upper-center**: directly in the legibility zone where text sits. Four images.
- **Human figures**: even tiny silhouettes pull attention. One was a person on a hilltop, two were "tiny figures on beach." Three images.
- **Iconic landmarks**: Golden Gate Bridge, Kirkjufell, recognizable city skylines (NYC kept as borderline). Place-specific scenes break the ambient feeling. Three images.
- **Slot-canyon / forest-canopy "all texture"**: zero calm zone for cards even though stunning photographs. Five images.
- **Foreground bokeh / leaf framing**: heavy edge detail pulls eye around the frame. Four images.
